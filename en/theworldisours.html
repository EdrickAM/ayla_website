Itinerary

Day 1

Hogona, Hogona! Train Time!

The adventure begins in our beloved Bengaluru! The group meets at 3:00 PM at Yeshwantpur Train Station (YPR), ready to board the comfortable 2° Class Air-conditioned train to Goa.

This is not just a journey; it is the transition from routine to holiday mode. Relax, read a book, watch your favorite series, or simply enjoy the scenery. A good chai and conversations with your new travel friends make it even more special.

Day 2

Arrival in Goa

We arrive in Goa in the morning and head straight to the resort, where a refreshing special tour day awaits.

8:30 AM: Enjoy a delicious Indian and Western breakfast while waiting for check-in.

8:15 / 9:00 AM: Optional morning exercises session at the rooftop, to start your day with energy.

The rest of the day is free! Choose from:

  • Relaxing at the pool
  • Walking on the beach
  • Joining a football or volleyball match
  • Lounging at the resort
  • Village Exploration
  • Free Cycling
  • Sunset on the beach

Day 3

Goa Beyond the Beaches

Spice Plantation, Goan Cooked Meal, Jeep Safari & Waterfalls!

Explore a different side of Goa at Tanshikar’s Organic Spice Farm, nestled in the lush Western Ghats. Wander through fragrant spice trails, discovering pepper, cinnamon, nutmeg, coffee, and cocoa while learning about traditional farming and sustainable practices.

Tanshikar’s Organic Spice Farm has received best organic farmer award from prime minister Modiji through Shri Shri agri institute trust on 11th of March 2016 in Delhi.

Continue the adventure with a jeep safari through untouched wilderness, leading to a hidden waterfall for a refreshing dip. End the day with a delicious Goan home-cooked meal made from fresh, local ingredients, blending nature, culture, and relaxation.

Day 4

Beach & Relaxation

8:15 / 9:00 AM: Optional morning workout to awaken the body and mind.

Spend the day your way—enjoy a relaxing beach walk, play football or volleyball on the sand, unwind by the pool.

After checkout, we will visit Fort Cabo de Rama, a historic site offering stunning coastal views and a serene atmosphere. This will be a special moment to reflect on our journey, and capture beautiful farewell photos. Surrounded by history and nature, it’s the perfect place to say goodbye and take one last glimpse of Goa’s breathtaking landscapes.

11:35 PM: Board the train back to Bangalore. Expected arrival at 7:00 AM (subject to train schedule adjustments).

Day 5

Farewell Goa

As we arrive in Bangalore this morning, we carry not just souvenirs, but a heart full of cherished memories and a phone filled with snapshots of laughter, adventure, and friendship. This journey has been more than just a trip—it’s been a celebration of new experiences, breathtaking landscapes, and the bonds we’ve built along the way.

Though we say goodbye to Goa, the spirit of travel stays with us. Let’s keep exploring, discovering, and creating unforgettable moments together. Shall we GOA with Diya again? Let’s make our next adventure happen!

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Itinerary and Schedule

The itinerary is fixed and requests to change the schedule or planned activities will not be accepted. Travelers must cooperate with the organizers to adhere to the established times, especially in the morning, ensuring that no one misses the scheduled experiences.

2. Personal Responsibility

Each traveler is responsible for their own safety. Avoid reckless or illegal behavior that may put you or the group at risk. A liability waiver must be signed upon boarding the vehicle, releasing Ayla Mundo from any unforeseen incidents, such as injuries, fatalities, theft, among others.

3. Unforeseen Situations

In cases of unforeseen circumstances, such as bad weather, landslides, the presence of wild animals, roadblocks by local authorities, or transportation delays, some activities may be canceled. When possible, the organizers will seek alternatives, but in some cases, a destination may be completely dropped.

4. Conduct During the Trip

The consumption of alcohol or tobacco during transfers and group activities is not permitted. Please use designated areas, so as not to disturb any group members. Mutual respect among travelers is expected. Avoid discussions on sensitive topics such as politics, religion, and social issues. Offensive language, harassment, heated arguments, or any form of aggression will result in the immediate exclusion of the traveler, without refund. In case of violation of Indian laws, local authorities may be contacted.

5. Accommodation

Twin rooms (with two single beds) are not available. In the case of double rooms, a double bed will be provided with separate bedding for two people. Check-in time is 2pm and check-out time is 11am.

Cancellation by Ayla Mundo

Ayla Mundo may cancel the trip if the minimum number of participants is not reached. The decision will be communicated up to 48 hours in advance. In the event of cancellation due to low enrollment, the traveler may choose between a full refund or a transfer to another package: the amount paid will be applied to another available trip, and if there is a price difference, the amount will be adjusted.

Cancellation due to External Factors

In cases of cancellation due to factors beyond our control, such as natural disasters, extreme weather conditions, government alerts, political unrest, roadblocks, train cancellations, or disease outbreaks (including Covid-19), the following conditions apply: no refund. The total amount paid may be used as credit towards another package on the same weekend or on future dates. Once participation in a new trip is confirmed, the same cancellation policies mentioned above apply.

Cancellation by the Traveler

Up to 3 days after payment: full refund of the amount paid. Less than 10 days before the trip begins: no refund or rescheduling will be provided, regardless of the reason (health, accident, Covid, family or work issues). Bookings made within 10 days of the trip start: no refund or rescheduling will be provided.

Date Change

It is possible to reschedule the trip for a future event up to 48 hours before the trip begins, allowed only once per booking. The cost of issued train tickets will be deducted.

Partial Payment (booking fee)

No refund or rescheduling will be provided for partial payments.

During or After the Trip

No refund will be provided under any circumstances.

Tickets

Train tickets are issued in advance and, in case of cancellation after issuance, the amount will be deducted from the refund.

Is this trip for you?

We want to create a group with easygoing people who see travel as an opportunity to connect with new places, cultures, and great company. This experience is designed for those who enjoy traveling without fuss—like a trip among friends, with an open mind and heart for new experiences.

If you are looking for intense partying, nightlife, or an emphasis on drinking, this might not be the right trip for you. Here, the goal is to relax, enjoy the beauty of Goa, share stories, and create genuine memories. Families, couples, solo travelers, and friends are all welcome—as long as the vibe is one of respect, positivity, and good energy!

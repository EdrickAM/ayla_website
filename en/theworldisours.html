Let's GOA!

With Diya

Get ready for a journey full of energy, laughter, and unforgettable memories!

Let's Goa is more than just a trip – it's a chance to disconnect from routine, meet amazing people, and explore the most authentic side of Goa. An experience like no other!

We begin with a 2A train journey, savoring every cup of Kaapi or chai while watching Karnataka’s landscapes transform into Goa’s. Mornings will be peaceful at the resort, alternating between a vibrant stroll through Agonda village, visits to historic landmarks, and pure relaxation on the beach – not to mention the stunning sunsets! Every detail has been carefully planned for you to enjoy, have fun, and experience the best of Goa with the lightness that only Ayla can provide. Shall we go together?

General Information

  • Date: March 20–24
  • Duration: 5 days / 4 nights
  • Participants: 15 travelers + 2 coordinators
  • Transport: 2A Train + AC Van
  • Hotel: 4-star resort
  • Meals: Breakfast included
  • Extras: Yoga class, Pilates, cycling, football, volleyball, and beach activities
  • Exclusive group: Maximum of 15 people

Itinerary

Day 1: Hogona, Hogona! Train Time!

The adventure begins in our Namma Bengaluru! The group meets at 3:00 PM at Yeshwantpur Train Station (YPR), ready to board the comfortable 2A class train to Goa.

This is not just a journey; it's the transition from routine to holiday mode. Relax, read a book, binge-watch your favorite series, or simply enjoy the scenery. A good chai and conversations with your new travel friends make it even more special.

Day 2: Arrival in Goa

We arrive in Goa in the morning and head straight to the resort, where a refreshing welcome drink awaits.

8:00 AM: Enjoy a delicious Indian and Western breakfast while waiting for check-in.

8:15 AM: Optional yoga or pilates session at the spa to start your day with energy.

The rest of the day is free! Choose from:

  • Relaxing at the pool
  • Walking on the beach
  • Joining a football or volleyball match
  • Lounging at the resort

5:00 PM: Village Exploration! A bicycle tour through Agonda, ending with a sunset on the beach.

Day 3: Exploring Agonda

8:00 - 10:30 AM: Slow mornings with a delicious breakfast.

9:00 AM: Optional morning workout at the spa.

11:00 AM: City Tour exploring Goa’s cultural and historical gems:

  • Spice Plantation – Dive into the aromas of Indian spices.
  • Cabo de Rama Fort – A breathtaking sea view.
  • Menezes Braganza House – One of Goa's oldest colonial mansions.
  • Goa Chitra Museum – A blend of art, culture, and history.

End the day watching the sunset at one of South Goa’s iconic beaches. Evening free for dinner and exploring.

Day 4: Beach & Relaxation

8:15 AM: Optional morning workout to wake up the body and mind.

Spend the day your way:

  • Relaxing beach walk
  • Optional surf lesson (extra cost)
  • Football & volleyball on the sand
  • Chilling at the pool
  • Spa treatments (extra cost, 15% discount for cash payments)

11:35 PM: Board the train back to Bangalore. Expected arrival at 7:00 AM (subject to train schedule adjustments).

Day 5: Farewell Goa

Morning arrival in Bangalore with a heart full of memories and a phone full of photos.
